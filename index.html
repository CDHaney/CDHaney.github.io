A Career in Software Engineering

An overview of the job and its prospects

Software engineering, otherwise known as software development, is a field in which workers apply engineering principles to the development of software. The job allows the workers to break the process of software design and creation into quantifiable, simpler pieces. Software developer, software tester, and application developer are some of the many jobs that software engineers can take, all of which rely on the programming and engineering experience of a software engineer. Beyond having a wide variety of jobs available, software engineers are in extremely high demand, with a projected growth rate of 24% in software engineering jobs from 2016 to 2026, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. This resource guide is designed to provide information on software engineering, such as the best graduate programs available for the field, professional organizations for software engineers, and resources for people interested in the field.
